WHAT IS CROWD?

Crowd is a social media platform where users are able to create and share events that will then be posted onto the crowd map. Visually the user will be able to track events on their map and RSVP to ones they are interested in.





OUR MISSION:

Crowd seeks to build a social media platform that not only connects people but also inspires users, informs and creates positive changes around your community.

We strive to grow our business through improving aspects of Crowd to ensure it is the best app it can be for our users.




ONGOING DEVELOPMENT:

Currently, Crowd is undergoing developments in implementing a friends list. This will be the best way to track friends and where they are in the 'Crowd'. Crowd would also like to implement popular tags in order to search for events the user is interested in therefore creating a more seamless user experience when using the app.
